--- layout: page nav-section: Why VBR? title: Paperless or Paper-lite? ---

Going “paperless” is a high aspiration – one which Virtualboardroom can help you achieve. But perhaps moving towards it through a “paper-lite” phase is a more realistic target.

Not everyone feels comfortable reading information electronically and some of our clients tell us many users still prefer paper, particularly when attending the actual board meeting.

That’s why the Boardpack tool in Virtualboardroom generates, and allows you to publish, both a print version and an e-version of your documents. This lets you reduce paper usage where individual board members elect to view electronically while still giving them choice in their preferred viewing method.

You can still improve your “green” credentials while continuing to use paper and this link to the Confederation of Paper Industries website provides a lot of useful information on the subject:

http://www.paper.org.uk/mythsandfacts/
