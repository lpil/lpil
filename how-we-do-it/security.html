Our clients range from international banking groups to local housing associations and charities. No matter the size of your organisation, the security of your information is just as much of a priority to us. Virtualboardroom is ISO 27001 certified and we have partnered with Rackspace, one of the world's leading hosting providers, who are SSAE 16 and ISO 27001 certified. Their data centre operations and procedures are ISAE 3402 Type II Service Organisation Control certified making them the most secure in the world.

Virtualboardroom has several core security features to keep your data safe and your mind at rest, including:

  • The sites are web based and accessed via SSL certificate
  • All data to and from the site has 256bit encryption
  • The data on the server is encrypted at rest as default using TDE (Transparent Data Encryption)
  • The data is backed up on a weekly full and daily differential timetable to tape. The tapes are fully encrypted and stored securely offsite
  • Users all have unique user names using forms based authentication methods
  • Passwords are classified as strong with a prompt to change every 90 days
  • The end board pack has the option to include AES 128bit encryption prior to publishing
  • An intrusion detection system (IDS) monitored by Alert Logic tracks all in and out bound activity
  • Regular vulnerability scanning
  • Penetration tested on at least an annual basis

Please contact us to obtain a copy of our full security and technical specification manual, or view our ISO 27001 certificate.
