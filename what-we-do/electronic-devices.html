Virtualboardroom® is the board portal of choice for organisations wanting to take advantage of mobile technology and access their information in real time. It can be used on any computer or mobile device, including a PC, laptop, smartphone, iPad or any other choice of tablet computer. All you need is an internet connection to download your documents, and then you can read and annotate them offline just like any other PDF. And with Virtualboardroom's extra layer of PDF encryption security, your documents will be completely safe.

Virtualboardroom is fully compatible with every tablet on the market, leaving the choice in your hands.

If you are using a tablet other than an iPad, contact us to find out more about our recommendations for third party apps and annotation tools.
